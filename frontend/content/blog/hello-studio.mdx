This is the first entry of the studio - a small public notebook attached to the
portfolio. The goal isn't to publish "content"; it's to keep a working log of
what I'm building and why.

## What lives here

The bento on the home page is the landing - selected work, a portrait, where
I am, what I'm into. The `/work` page is the full catalog (main case studies
plus a denser grid of mini projects). `/about` is the deeper introduction with
a scroll-driven 360° portrait I rendered with AI. And this - `/blog` - is the
notebook.

## What I'll write about

- Building RAG systems that don't need a paid API in the loop.
- Practical computer vision - what holds up outside the demo.
- The small craft choices that make a product feel fast.
- Lessons from working at the intersection of AI/ML, software, and data.

Posts will be short, opinionated, and honest. I'd rather ship a 400-word
note than sit on a 2,000-word essay.

Subscribe via RSS when it lands. Or just bookmark `/blog` - that works too.
